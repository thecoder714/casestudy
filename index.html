Research on Google Lens and MobileNet


Conclusion: According to our findings, google lens has correctly identified the below 10 objects. On the contrary, the MobileNet framework has not been able to identify most of the objects. We recommend you to not use the MobileNet model and use the Google Lens API instead.

NOTE: The MobileNet model seems to work very well in a few cases. However, the Google Lens API seems to work incredibly well in most cases. This may be due to lighting conditions or how exactly the photograph was taken. The results may vary depending on the conditions.
